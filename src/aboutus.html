About Us

Pai Bakery was started in 1990 as a family run bussiness under the firm name Ratans.
Initially, we were manufacturing only bread. Gradually got diversified into cakes and other snacks.
We are mainly into wholesaling of bakery products.Freshly prepared products are transported through four wheeler vans and reach retailers like kirana stores , sweet marts and superstores.
Managing partners Sri. Venkatesh Pai and Smt. Jyotsna Pai have attended amny bakery owner courses to streamline production and management.
Mrs.Pai has recieved the BEST BUSSINESS PLAN award after participating in programme organised by Goldman Sachs and ISB Hyderabad.
The retailers are atleast 1000 shops of Belagavi.
